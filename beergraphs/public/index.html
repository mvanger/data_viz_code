Beers Above Replacement is BeerGraphs' proprietary measure of a beer's quality. It is loosely based on baseball's Wins Above Replacement metric, which reflects the number of additional wins a team has amassed relative to their expected number of wins had a replacement level player been brought in.

BAR uses ratings and number of check-ins as measures of performance and popularity. It reflects a beer's performance relative to its style, since ratings vary widely between an IPA, and a stout, for example. It also attempts to take a beer's distribution (e.g. national vs. local) into account by logging the number of check-ins.

--

Style+ is similar to BAR but does not take number of check-ins (popularity) into account. It simply weights a beer's rating relative to its style, but is a good way of finding lesser-known beers.

--

ABV is Alcohol By Volume.
